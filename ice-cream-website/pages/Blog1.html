Fun Facts About Ice Cream

Did you know these interesting facts about ice cream?

  • Vanilla is the most popular ice cream flavor in the United States.
  • The world's largest ice cream sundae weighed over 24 tons.
  • The ice cream cone was invented at the 1904 World's Fair in St. Louis, Missouri.
